How "living data" works here

LEFT pane is the editor. RIGHT pane reads via the engine's /subscribe long-poll. When you nudge a condition slider on the LEFT, the SPA recomputes ΔG + K_eq, commits a new version to nDB, and the RIGHT pane sees the update arrive from the engine — the records that share any compound with the edited one pulse yellow.

The engine has no triggers — propagation is application-layer. What you're seeing is the same wire mechanism that would power a multi-tab or multi-process reactive system.
